<p>Welcome 2024 Secondary 1 Admirals to our Admiralty Family!</p>
<p>Our Secondary 1 Admirals were welcomed into the Admiralty Family through
a whole-week of Orientation Programme from 2 to 5 Jan 2024. Friendships
were established through icebreakers, and team-bonding games facilitated
by the student leaders and form teachers. Our Sec 1 Admirals participated
enthusiastically in the school tour and CCA Experience, where experiential
learning, games, and quizzes were incorporated.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The main highlight of the programme was the “Orion Aspirational Activity”,
where each class was tasked to brainstorm and perform the school cheer
on an innovative way. The Sec 1 Admirals demonstrated the R3ich in LIGHTS
values as they overcame this fun challenge together. The programme concluded
with the Sec 1 Admirals singing the school song and school cheer with gusto
- a clear evidence that Sec 1 Admirals have developed a strong sense of
belonging through these activities.</p>
