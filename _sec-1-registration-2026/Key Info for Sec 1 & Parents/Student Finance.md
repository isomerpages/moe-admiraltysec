<p><strong><u>MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS)</u></strong>&nbsp;
<br>
<br><strong><u>Application for MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS)</u></strong><u>&nbsp;</u>
<br>The objective of the MOE FAS is to provide financial assistance to Singaporean
students who need financial assistance for their studies. More information
on the MOE FAS is available <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance" class="Hyperlink SCXW141121221 BCX0" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><u>here</u></a>.&nbsp;
<br>
<br>You may apply for the FAS via Parent Gateway (under ‘Services’) at <a href="https://form.gov.sg/68f6ebe3b483709b618276c7" class="Hyperlink SCXW141121221 BCX0" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><u>MOE FAS/ISB/SPED FAS Application Home Page | FormSG</u></a>.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
<br>
<br><strong><u>School-Based FAS (SBFAS)</u></strong>&nbsp;
<br>Students whose MOE FAS applications are unsuccessful will be automatically
considered for SBFAS.&nbsp;
<br>
<br>The criteria are a monthly gross household income between $4,001 and $5,500
or per capita income between $1,001 and $1,375.&nbsp;
<br>
</p>
<p>The assistance for SBFAS includes full subsidy for school fees, free textbooks
and attire, and transport subsidies (on application).&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>All Parents / Students who intend to apply for the MOE FAS or SBFAS should wait for the confirmation of FAS status before purchasing any books or uniform.</strong>&nbsp;</p>
